Method, briefly

A compliance stress test, not a universal model ranking.

The runner sent one byte-identical prompt to 18 models through OpenRouter, testing every reasoning effort each model advertised. Requests ran sequentially in a fixed shuffled order.

The scorer awarded 100 deterministic points across JSON structure, quantitative work, assumptions and evidence, creative requirements, and breadth. Successful visible answers are public; raw streams and provider metadata are not.

One sample per setting cannot establish stable latency or answer quality. Factual correctness and citation quality require a separate blinded review.
